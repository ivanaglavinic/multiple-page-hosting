Ice cream has ancient origins, with early forms dating back to China around 200 BC, where a frozen mixture of milk and rice was enjoyed. The concept spread to Europe, and by the 17th century, Italian and French chefs refined ice cream as we know it today. It became popular in the U.S. in the 18th century, with the invention of modern ice cream machines in the 19th century revolutionizing its production.
